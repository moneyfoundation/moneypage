WHAT MAKES Money DIFFERENT?

We engineered Money to be a digital version of cash. When sending Money, you choose whether the transaction is private or public.

Your Money are stored in a digital wallet that you own and control. Transactions are anonymous "from the wallet" meaning you don't have to trust a third party to make the transaction private.
